Accounting

Por Ngor Dor 50 (ภงด. 50)

Por Ngor Dor 50 (ภงด. 50) is a tax form used in Thailand for corporate income tax filing. It is specifically for annual tax returns and is used by companies to report their income, calculate their tax liability, and summarize their financial activities for the fiscal year.

Por Ngor Dor 30 (ภงด. 30)

If an individual in Thailand engages in business activities and has annual sales exceeding 1.8 million baht, they are required to register for Value Added Tax (VAT). Once registered, they need to file Por Ngor Dor 30 (ภงด. 30) to report their VAT transactions.

Por Ngor Dor 90 (ภงด. 90)

Por Ngor Dor 90 (ภงด. 90) for individuals with additional sources of income, such as freelance work or rental income.

Por Ngor Dor 94 (ภงด. 94)

Por Ngor Dor 94 (ภงด. 94) is a tax form used in Thailand for the semi-annual personal income tax filing. It is primarily used by individuals to report their income for the first half of the year and to make an advance payment on their annual personal income tax liability.
